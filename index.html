About

Bridging elite biology training with real-world AI engineering.

My career began in molecular biology and genomics, where I trained in gene regulation and functional genomics and published first-author work in Nature and Nature Biotechnology. Over time, I moved toward machine learning, cloud platforms, and data engineering—drawn by the challenge of making complex scientific systems reliable, scalable, and useful in practice.

Today, I work at the intersection of biology, machine learning, and systems engineering. I care not only about model performance, but about whether a system can be evaluated rigorously, deployed safely, and trusted in high-stakes environments.
